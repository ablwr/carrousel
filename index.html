Carrousel

is a colloquium dedicated to unpacking the non-representational referent of the utopia itself as manifested through media, of the taming unknown territory, of the aid of electronic data processing, of minds behind hypertext and the Internet, designing for bots, managing through metadata, ontologies for the web, "aol keywords," the meaning behind domain name systems, shift from fantasy-hollywood to reality-filming, vision viewfinders, calendar without numbers.
